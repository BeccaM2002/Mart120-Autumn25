2nd Page - Story - Bloody Clock

Kakooo Kakooo “ Harry… HARRY! - Shut up that bloody damn clock before I shove it up your asses ears!!!” mine wife shreels.

Tick

Tick

Kookoo kookoo

“HARRY!!!!!!”

Thump

I opened me veyes with a snort, ”yes dear!”

Tick

Tick

“You god awful bloody clock with … punctuality lacking, your fulfilled melodi to be shove up my asses ears by yur mum before me bed to death!” I spit and mutter.

Me hands shakin’ from the toil of 30 springs nets pulled.

“You useless piece of cogs, long loved only to end up in bed with a man old’n dead,” I chortle.

I look yond to the hills aboun. The sails in the east harbor bound tine. The clock makin shop two down from the dock. The gilz at sils & mine wife pie a mighty treat fur lucky servivers.

Me veye shutter, the salt tinge te tongue.

Tick

My scruf hackles.

“Harry!!!!! Fix the bloody arse …. Me Pie,” me old wife shrills.

Varied the soun’d of birds and bays shake the floor timbers.

Me mouths corners sink into my cheeks as a child’s cackle breasts the tongue. “Yes Dear.”
